My Bookstore
Site Name:

The name “My Bookstore” was chosen because it is simple, easy to remember, and clearly communicates the purpose of the site. It immediately lets visitors know that the website is an online bookstore where they can browse and purchase books.

Site Purpose:

The purpose of My Bookstore is to provide an online platform for browsing and purchasing books. The site offers sections for new releases, bestsellers, and detailed book information, including descriptions, author details, and reviews.

Scenarios:

- Scenario 1:

A visitor wants to find books from a specific genre, such as science fiction or mystery. They expect to quickly filter the catalog by categories, authors, or popularity to find relevant titles.

- Scenario 2:

A visitor is interested in a particular book and wants to see detailed information, including a synopsis, author details, and user reviews, before deciding to purchase or add it to their reading list.
